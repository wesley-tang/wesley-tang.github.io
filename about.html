About Me

I am a 3rd year computer science student at the University of Calgary with a concentration in Human Computer Interactions. Creating is my thing, whether it be with the strokes of a keyboard or the lens of a camera, I love to make! Some of my hobbies include coding, gaming, drawing, engaging in spirited discourse regarding the human condition and volunteering at the local animal shelter. My favourite things include dogs, watermelon, my friends and unecessarily and frequently ostentationsly verbose and eloquent prose.
